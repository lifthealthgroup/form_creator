Generate Assessments Tool

This application allows you to efficiently generate assessment forms. You must use the provided Excel (.xlsx) document. Blank columns will not have assessments generated.

Once you've filled out your assessments, click on the "Choose Files" button to select your files, and then hit the "Upload" button to process them.
